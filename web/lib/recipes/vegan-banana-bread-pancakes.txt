title: Vegan Banana Bread Pancakes

ingredients:
- 2 cups whole wheat flour
- 4 1/2 tsp baking powder
- 1/2 tsp kosher salt
- 2 tsp ground cinnamon
- 2 tsp sugar
- 2 Flax eggs
- 1 Mashed bananas
- 2 cups Plant milk
- 2 tsp vanilla extract
- Walnuts for topping
- Cooking spray


directions:
1. Mix all the dry ingredients in a bowl. Add the wet ingredients to the mixing bowl and mix well with a spoon until there are no more dry spots and clumps. However, don't over-mix.
2. Heat a large skillet on medium heat. Lightly spray it with oil or cooking spray to coat
3. Pour 1/4 cup of pancake batter, and then top with walnuts. 
4. When the pancake starts to bubble, give it a few minutes to settle, and when the edges begin to set, flip the pancakes.
5. Repeat with the remainder of the batter. Makes 12 pancakes.
6. Top with sliced bananas, walnuts, and your favorite sweetener.
7. Enjoy!
