title: Oatmeal Raisin Cookies Full of Goodness and Flavor

ingredients:
- 1 mashed banana 
- 1/3 cup maple syrup 
- 1/3 cup coconut oil (melted or olive oil)
- 1 flax egg
- 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract 
- 1/2 cup oat flour 
- 2 tablespoons wheat bran, wheat germ, or almond meal 
- 1 teaspoon baking powder 
- 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon 
- 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg 
- 1/4 teaspoon salt 
- 1 cup rolled oats 
- 2 tablespoons of ground flax seeds
- 1/2 cup raisins 

directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
2. Combine wet ingredients and dry ingredients (except flax seeds, oats, and raisins) in two separate bowls.
3. Pour dry ingredients into wet ingredients. Combine and add in flax seeds, oats, & raisins. 
4. Scoop cookie batter onto the baking sheet (2-3 tbs dollops) and place them 2 inches apart.
5. Bake for 10-12 minutes until lightly brown on the edges. Allow sitting on the baking pan for 5-10 mins before removing them to cool.
6. Enjoy!
