title: Wholesome Kamut Breakfast Pancakes and Porridge

ingredients:
- 1 cup Kamut berries or Kamut flour
- Â¼ cup Tahini butter  
- 1 cup plant-based milk  
- 1 Tsp Cinnamon
- 1 Tsp Ginger 
- 1-2 Cloves ground up
- 1 Tbs Coconut Sugar  
- 1 tbsp Grapeseed oil
- 1/2 tsp Baking powder

directions:
1. Add Kamut berries to a blender and blend until a fine flour.
2. In a separate bowl, mix the Kamut flour with the Tahini butter, milk, cinnamon, ginger, and cloves.
3. Add the baking powder and sugar and mix well.
4. Heat vegan butter or oil in a pan and pour 1/4 cup of the batter into the pan.
5. Flip after a few minutes and cook until both sides are golden brown.
6. Serve warm with maple syrup or honey and bananas.
