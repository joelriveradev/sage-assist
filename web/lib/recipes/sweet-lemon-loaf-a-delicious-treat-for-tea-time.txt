title: Sweet Lemon Loaf - A Delicious Treat for Tea Time

ingredients:
- 2 cups white flour
- 3/4 cup white sugar or similar sugar alternative of your choice (another substitute may affect the final outcome)
- 1 tablespoon baking powder
- 1/2 teaspoon of salt
- Zest of 1 lemon 
- 1 and 1/2 cups thick coconut milk
- 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla essence or flavor
- 2 tablespoons fresh or bottled lemon juice


directions:
1. Set the oven to 350F.
2. In a bowl, add all of the dry ingredients and mix with a wooden spoon.
3. Add the lemon zest and remaining ingredients and mix well. 
4. Place in a 9-inch baking pan and bake in the oven for 40 minutes.
5. When the loaf is done, mix the glaze and pour it over the loaf while it is still warm.
