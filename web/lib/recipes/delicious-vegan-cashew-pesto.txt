title: Delicious Vegan Cashew Pesto

ingredients:
- 1 cup (150 g) raw cashews (soaked for at least four hours, or boiled for 15 minutes, then drained).
- 10 tbsp. hot water
- Approximately 6 cups (120 g) fresh basil (leaves and stems) 
- 1/3 cup (50 g) nutritional yeast flakes
- 6 cloves roasted garlic
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 2 tbsp lemon juice
- Vegan parmesan cheese

directions:
1. Place the cashews into a blender or food processor, and blend until you have a fine powder.
2. Add the water, a little at a time, and continue to process until the cashews form a thick cream.
3. Add the rest of the ingredients, and blend again for 30-60 seconds, until you have a coarse paste.
4. Serve with your favorite pasta and top with grated parmesan and sliced sundried tomatoes.
