title: Nutritious Sikil Pak Pumpkin Seed Dip

ingredients:
- 1/2 Cup pumpkin seeds 
- 1 Roma tomato
- 1/4 Onion
- 1/4 Cup Cilantro
- 1 Slice of Habanero (optional)
- 1/4-1/2 tsp salt

directions:
1. Toast pepita seeds in a pan until lightly roasted, then transfer to a plate to cool. 
2. While the pepita seeds are cooling, roast the tomato and onion in a pan until charred or over an open flame. 
3. Blend pumpkin seeds until a flour, transfer to a bowl and set aside
4. Blend the tomato and onion with salt and pepper, with just a tiny splash of water to help blend. 
5. Add the tomato and onion puree to a bowl and start mixing in the pumpkin seed flour bit by bit until desired consistency. 
6. Enjoy with veggies or chips!
