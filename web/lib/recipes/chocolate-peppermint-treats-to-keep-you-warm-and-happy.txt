title: A Quick and Easy DIY Oat Milk Recipe

ingredients:
- 2 Tbs chia seeds
- 1 Tbs cocoa or carob powder (carob will be less sweet)
- 1 Tbs hemp seeds
- 1 Heaping tbs oats
- 1 Tbs maple syrup
- 1/4 Tsp peppermint extract
- 1/2 Cup plant-based milk

directions:
1. Mix everything, transfer it to a mason jar or Tupperware and let sit overnight.
2. Top with coconut cream (the top of the coconut milk can).
3. Enjoy!
