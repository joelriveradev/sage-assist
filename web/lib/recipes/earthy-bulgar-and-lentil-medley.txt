title: Earthy Bulgar and Lentil Medley

ingredients:
- 1 Cup Brown or Green Lentils
- 1 Cup Bulgur Wheat
- 4 Cups Vegetable Stock
- 1 Punnet of brown mushrooms
- 1 Head of Broccoli
- Half of a Leek
- 1 Red Onion
- Handful of Kale leaves

directions:
1. Rinse the brown lentils and bulgur thoroughly and add them to a pot.
2. Add 4 cups of vegetable stock and bring to a boil. Let it simmer on low heat for about 40 minutes, or until soft and fluffy.
3. Slice the mushrooms and broccoli and place them on a tray topped with a tablespoon of olive oil and a sprinkle of salt.
4. Roast in the oven for about 30 minutes at 290 F.
5. Slice the onion and leeks and place them in a pan with heated oil. Keep stirring while adding a teaspoon of water every couple of minutes until the onions have caramelized.
6. Place all the ingredients on a warm plate, and top off with some fresh kale leaves.
